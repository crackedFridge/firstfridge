Verifying a summary of achievements

Using eCampus - FlexNow - Summary of achievements - students can generate a summary of their achievements to date as a PDF file.

Here, you can verify the validity of these files by downloading the original version as a PDF file. This must match the copy you received.

To do this, enter the ID and password you receive on the printout. Then click on "Check". The summary of achievements will either open in a new window or be saved on your hard drive as a document, depending on your browser settings.
